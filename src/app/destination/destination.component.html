To attend Matches of the 2018 FIFA World Cup Russia™ you need to obtain a FAN ID. Please visit www.fan-id.ru for more information. You can apply for your FAN ID after paying for your Tickets and receiving your Ticket confirmation email. Please also inform your Guests and Companions about the requirement of obtaining a FAN ID. The FAN ID has been introduced at the request of the Russian authorities for all fans visiting Matches at the 2018 FIFA World Cup Russia™. FIFA does not assume any liability or responsibility in respect of the application, issuance and use of the FAN ID.
